Admission Procedure

Online Admission Application Guide

Admission Policy

New enrollees at St. Augustine Academy of Pampanga, Inc. are expected to accept and uphold the school policy and objectives and to submit to its rules and regulations.

The school reserves the right to admit only those students/pupils who meet the school's requirements. Academic ability as well as character traits of the learner applicants is taken into consideration.

Application Steps

1

Student Online Registration

1.1 Open an Internet browser and go to https://zozo617.github.io/saap-demo/admission-procedure.html.
1.2 Read policy, steps, and check for the documents.
1.3 Click “Online Application”.
1.4 Complete the Online Application Form.
1.5 A temporary ID will be sent to you after you complete the application form, use that to the MySAAP Login Page to be able to see your application status.
2

Pay the Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable 200 pesos application fee at the SAAP Cashier ONLY.
(Note: The specific instructions for the payment will be sent to your MySAAP account.)

3

Wait for Test Schedule

Wait for the test permit, test schedule, and specific instructions for the on-site in your MySAAP account.

4

Examination Results & Interview

Receive the examination results through your MySAAP account, and intake interviews will follow on a given schedule.

List of Documents for Uploading

PSGS / JHS Requirements

  • 2x2 photo with name tag
  • GMRC Certificate (original)
  • Recent Report Card (F138) photocopy
  • Copy of Application Fee Receipt

Grants / ESC / Financial Assistance (Gr7)

  • Photocopy of Grade 6 Report Card
  • Certificate of Ranking
  • 2x2 pictures with name tag
  • Photocopy PSA Birth Certificate
  • Latest ITR / Tax Exemption OR Employment Certificate with Compensation OR Municipal Certification of Unemployment for both parents/guardians.

SHS Requirements

  • 2x2 photo with name tag
  • GMRC Certificate (original)
  • Recent Report Card (F138) photocopy
  • Copy of Learner's Reference Number (LRN)
  • ESC Certificate / QVR Certificate
  • NCAE COR (photocopy)
  • Copy of Application Fee Receipt
