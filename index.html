Frequently Asked Questions

SAAP is located along the Angeles-Porac-Floridablanca-Dinalupihan Road, in Poblacion, Floridablanca, Pampanga, 2006, situated right beside the St. Joseph the Worker Parish Church.

Yes, St. Augustine Academy of Pampanga, Inc. (SAAPI) is a private Catholic school owned and operated by the Archdiocese of San Fernando.

Yes, SAAP does not discriminate against the admission and employment of non-Catholics, provided they are willing to follow the school's rules and regulations, Catholic teachings, and practices.

Jewelry is not allowed for either boys or girls, except for a wristwatch. Nail polish and make-up are strictly prohibited; however, light make-up is permitted for female Senior High School (SHS) students only.

Cellphones/gadgets are allowed if they will be officially used for emergency or academic purposes WITH a permit signed by the parent/guardian. The permit signed by the guardian will be presented to the adviser, subject teacher, grade level coordinator, POD, and the principal for approval.

Various scholarships and service grants are available to deserving students. Please inquire at the Lingap Center or contact wecare@saap.edu.ph for details.
